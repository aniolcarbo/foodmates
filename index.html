Introducing Foodmates

Foodmates is an app where eating healthy starts by looking at what's in front of you.

We offer a huge variety of recipes based on food lifestyles, cultures and, most importantly, the food in your fridge.

Explore

Discover the best recipes from your favourite cultures! Mediterranean, Japanese, Mexican, Chinese... everything! Let's expand our culinary taste beyond the boundaries.

Community

For the beginning of time, food has brought us all together. That's why, we offer a social network to share your recipes, so we can talk, share and be passionate about our meals. You might make a new friend, or more ;)

Flexibility

Our algorithm gives you recipes that complement you better. Not only on taste, but on the products you tend to have or the ones you already own.
